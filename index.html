A creative, relaxed studio environment
...only minutes
from Belfast City Centre

Multi Track Recording combines analogue and digital, hardware and software for the best possible results across all musical genres.

Fully Equipped Practice Space open 7 days per week 10am to late. Block bookings and late sessions welcome. Free tea and coffee and use of communal kitchen area. Service lift for transporting equipment. Onsite free parking

Earthmusic client base has been predominantly the local Northern Ireland music scene and has welcomed solo singer songwriters, bands, promoters and indie labels covering a wide spectrum of musical genres and also hosts Trinity College London's 'Rock N Pop' exams.

Services

Muliti Track Recording

Recording all together for that authentic 'live' feel or separation for the extra polish, or a bit of both worlds, laying down 'guide' tracks overdubbing and building from the ground up. All of that shiz.

Song Arrangement/Production

Solo songwriters have your songs taken to full arrangement and production with experienced musician/producer providing all instrumentation and programming with over 20 years experience with many genres of music and previous work in London and the US.

Mastering

Need your tracks mastered for release ? Full spec mastering with all the associated data required for final glass master. And we always go easy on the compressors.

Drum Recording

Home Set Up ? But don't have the space or experience to record 'live' drums ? Come in we'll record what you need and you take the files and finish the job at home

Mixes and Re-Mixes

Bring all your stems/individual tracks and have your songs mixed back through our lovely analog and valve console and get that 'warmth' we all crave.

Rehearsal Space

Fully equipped comfortable practice space comes complete with inhouse Mapex Venus Drumkit, Fender, Torque, Hughes&Kettner, Laney and Line 6 Guitar and Bass Combo Amps, Full Range PA and floor monitors microphones and stands.

EarthMusic

Started in 2002 Earthmusic has moved around a bit before settling where the studio currently resides just five minutes from Belfast City Centre, it is the second commercial studio venture that I have managed having first co-owned Discipline Studios in Tottenham North London in the 90's.

Born in Belfast I moved to London with local band Chat Quixote to pursue the 'rockstar' dream and stayed in the big smoke for almost ten years spending time in subsequent London bands IF THIS WAS YOU and lastly TO BE BLONDE before heading to San Francisco where I managed to do some production and engineer work with various entities before returning to my native Belfast a married person.
